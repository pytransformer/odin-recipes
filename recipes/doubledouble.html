Double Double

Description

In-N-Out's Double-Double stands as an iconic testament to the West Coast's burger culture, boasting two succulent 100% pure beef patties perfectly grilled to perfection. This classic creation is aptly named for its double dose of deliciousness, with two slices of American cheese melding seamlessly over the juicy patties. Complemented by fresh, crisp lettuce, hand-sliced tomatoes, and In-N-Out's signature spread, this burger offers a symphony of flavors and textures. The simplicity of its ingredients allows the quality of each element to shine, resulting in a mouthwatering experience that has solidified the Double-Double as a beloved favorite among In-N-Out enthusiasts, delivering a gratifying and satisfying bite with every order.

Ingredients

  • Dill Pickle Relish
  • Onion
  • Paprika
  • Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup
  • Mayo
  • 25-30% Fat Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Olive Oil
  • American Cheese
  • Iceberg Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Mince the relish and onions into fine pieces.
  2. Stir with Paprika, vinegar, salt, mustard, ketchup, and mayo.
  3. Let the mixture sit.
  4. Ground the beef.
  5. Make patties.
  6. Toast the buns with butter until golden brown.
  7. Cook the patties until well done.
  8. Spread the sauce on the bottom bun.
  9. Combine the burger.
  10. Serve hot!
