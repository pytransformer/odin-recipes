Hash Browns

Description

Hashbrowns, revered as a breakfast staple, embody the essence of morning indulgence with their golden-brown exterior and tender, fluffy interior. This beloved dish stands as a timeless testament to breakfast culture, showcasing grated potatoes expertly fried to crispy perfection. Each bite delivers a symphony of textures, from the satisfying crunch of the outer layer to the soft, comforting center. Seasoned with a delicate balance of salt and pepper, hashbrowns offer a delightful burst of flavor with every mouthful. Whether served as a side dish or the star of the breakfast plate, hashbrowns captivate taste buds with their simplicity and savory allure. Their irresistible appeal has solidified them as a cherished favorite among breakfast enthusiasts, promising a gratifying start to the day with every savory bite.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes, shredded
  • ½ medium onion, finely chopped
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup oil for frying
  • salt and pepper to taste
